Prestige Cars

From ALEXANDERS PRESTIGE

Founded in 2005 by friends Andrew North and Alexander Brimelow, Alexanders Prestige is one of the UK's leading dealerships of prestige cars & offers a hand-picked collection of luxury cars, 4x4s and supercars for sale. Possessing some of the finest models from the most iconic names in the world of prestige motors, Alexanders Prestige offers an award-winning, personal service that delivers the very best in luxury specialist automotive retail.

Everything we do at Alexanders Prestige has been tailored to be of the highest standard. From the moment you step into our heated North Yorkshire showroom - one of the largest in the country - to browse the selection of prestige used cars for sale, you can anticipate a welcoming experience in an environment that is perfect for discovering your dream car.

In fact, it’s our aim to ensure that our friendly and knowledgeable team will be able to support you throughout the entire purchasing process. Whether it’s showing you around the selection of models from such renowned manufacturers as Aston Martin, used Bentley, Land Rover, used Porsche, Range Rover, and Ferrari, or helping you secure the car finance package that best suits you, they’ll be happy to assist you from the moment you choose your model to the moment you drive away.

What’s more, no matter if you're from London or Scotland, we at Alexanders Prestige can even work with you to sell your car. Whether you have a pre-owned Rolls Royce you wish to exchange, or a thrilling performance car you’re looking to sell, we can help manage the sale, leaving you to simply enjoy the process of selecting a new model.

Our range of nearly new cars and pre-owned cars is updated regularly as we take ownership of new stock. With an expert team of technicians in our on-site garage and a meticulous workshop vehicle detailing process, we're positive that you'll find something that takes your fancy from our lineup of prestige cars at Alexanders.
